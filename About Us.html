About Us

Welcome to Football Manager! Our game is designed to provide an immersive football management experience that combines real-world fitness tracking with engaging gameplay. Our mission is to promote healthy living, inclusivity, and sustainability through the power of gaming.

Our team is passionate about creating a game that not only entertains but also makes a positive impact on the world. We believe in the power of sports to bring people together and create a better future.
